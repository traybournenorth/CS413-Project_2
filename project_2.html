Instructions

When in the menu screen, use the W and S keys to navigate through the menu options ( Press enter to move to next screen ).

When choosing your avatar, use the A and D keys to pick ( Press space to pick avatar and start game ). Use the W, A, S, D keys to

move your avatar next to the answers of each question. If you are right, you will gain 100 points. If you are wrong you will gain 0 points. Good luck!

Click the buttons to play or pause the audio.
